Lakewood Baptist Church

Directions

 

The church is located at 14321 Detroit in Lakewood, between Olive and Lincoln.  Get off I-90 at the Warren Rd. / West 150th St. exit, go north on Warren Rd.  Cross Madison, among other streets, and turn right onto Franklin.  Take Franklin to Lincoln and turn left.  Lincoln dead-ends at Detroit, the church and an entrance to the parking lot is on your right.
